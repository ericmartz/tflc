The Coaching Staff

Eric Smith is the founder of The EKS Group, LLC, and creator of The Financial Literacy Coach and The Money Game. With over 25 years experience in the financial industry, Eric has the expertise needed to teach young students how to succeed financially, no matter which career they choose to enter after college.

For twenty five years, he has been blessed with a successful career in the banking industry. During the nineties, Eric was one of the highest producing mortgage originators in the metro Atlanta area originating over one half billion dollars of residential mortgages. As a lender and manager, he has originated and managed nearly one billion dollars of loans, making him one of the highest producing commercial lenders in the metro Atlanta market.
Read More...

As the youngest executive officer of a regional banking company, Eric has been involved in the credit review of thousands, many of whom have been professional athletes. As a cash flow and credit consultant, Eric’s clients have included small business owners, real estate developers, commercial contractors, politicians, and professional athletes.

Eric graduated with honors from Georgia State University and from the Graduate Banking School at LSU. He understands the importance of community service and has served previously as a Director of the Midwest Georgia Homebuilders Association and as Finance Chair for the Boy Scouts. He currently serves as a board member and Vice Chairman of the Coweta County Water Authority as well as the treasurer of his local church. He resides in Coweta County, Georgia with his wife, two children, two dogs, one cat and thirty seven chickens. He is an avid outdoorsman who enjoys hunting, fishing, and amateur farming. He has a work ethic that he describes as compulsive and makes it a point to never take himself too seriously.

Eric has made numerous presentations across the country to thousands of NCAA student athletes:

Guest Life Skill presenter at the second annual 2012 NFL-NCAA Life Skills Development Summit-Dallas,Texas
Presenter 2012 N4A National Convention Buffalo, NY
Presenter 2012 NACADA National Convention Nashville, TN
Presenter at the NFL rookie camps of the Seattle Seahawks, Miami Dolphins, and Carolina Panthers
Guest Presenter at the 2013 NBA Draft
