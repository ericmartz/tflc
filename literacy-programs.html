Financial Literacy Programs

Training Camps

Training Camps are financial literacy presentations and include The Money Game series. Camps are conducted on campus through an informative, interactive and memorable format that introduces and reinforces general financial concepts and principles. The training camps typically include up to 60 minutes of PowerPoint instruction followed by up to 60 minutes of Q&A.

Content of Training Camps can include:
  • Spending, Saving, and Retirement Planning
  • Understanding Credit
  • Basics of Investing
  • Identity Theft
  • Managing Debt
  • Importance of Budgeting
  • Goal Setting
Please contact The Financial Literacy Coach for additional information, pricing, or scheduling.

"The Next Step" Program

The Next Step is a sport-specific program intended for potential NFL, MLB, and NBA players. This program includes one-on-one student athlete consulting and up to 8 total hours of financial instruction. This includes:
  • Pre-Draft Preparedness
  • Cash Flow Management
  • Understanding Compensation and Benefits
  • How to Select a Financial Advisor
  • Increasing Chances for Financial Success
  • Avoiding Financial Pitfalls
  • Maximizing your Professional Career
Please contact The Financial Literacy Coach for pricing, details or scheduling.

Full Season Pass

The Full Season Pass is the ultimate in providing year round support for students. The Full Season Pass includes up to 4 individual Training Camps as well as 2 Next Step programs. This package involves a total of 6 on-campus visits from The Financial Literacy Coaching staff. Coach Smith will personally develop a customized training program that addresses the financial literacy of students while providing professional preparedness for future NFL, MLB, and NBA athletes. Benefits of the Full Season Pass include:
  • Value Pricing
  • Priority Scheduling
  • Year Round Financial Educational Resources
  • Athlete Development/Recruiting Distinction
Please contact The Financial Literacy Coach for Full Season Pass information, pricing, or scheduling.
